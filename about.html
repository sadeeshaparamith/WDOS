Our Story

At Market Medley, we take pride in offering a unique shopping experience that combines convenience, quality, and community. Since our establishment, we have been committed to serving our customers with the finest products and exceptional services.

Branches

Market Medley Fresh Heights

Location: 123 Greenway Avenue Fresh Heights, FL 32801

Nestled in the bustling neighborhood of Fresh Heights, this branch of Market Medley is known for its vibrant, farm-to-table produce section. Shoppers can find a wide array of organic fruits and vegetables sourced from local farms, ensuring the freshest ingredients for your meals. The store also features a dedicated section for artisanal cheeses, freshly baked breads, and a vast selection of international delicacies.

Market Medley Urban Green

Location: 456 Central Boulevard Downtown, FL 33130

Located in the heart of downtown, Market Medley Urban Green offers a modern shopping experience with an emphasis on sustainability. This eco-friendly branch is designed with green initiatives in mind, featuring a rooftop garden that supplies fresh herbs and greens to the store. Urban Green also provides a variety of eco-conscious products, bulk food options to reduce packaging waste, and a community space for cooking classes and workshops on sustainable living.

Market Medley Coastal Breeze

Location: 789 Oceanfront Drive Coastal Breeze, FL 33404

Situated near the picturesque coastline, Market Medley Coastal Breeze captures the essence of seaside living. This branch boasts a fantastic seafood department, offering a daily catch of locally sourced fish and shellfish. Alongside the seafood, shoppers can explore an extensive wine and spirits section perfect for pairing with any meal. Coastal Breeze is also known for its beach-inspired caf√©, where patrons can enjoy fresh smoothies, salads, and light bites while taking in the ocean view.
